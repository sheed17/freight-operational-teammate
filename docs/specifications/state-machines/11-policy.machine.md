# Machine M11 — Policy

*Registry: `registry.md`. Entity: `entities/14-policy.md`. Lifecycle: Target Spec §12.11; ADR-010.*

> The brief names "narrowed / suspended / invalid". ### **No new states:** *narrowed* = an `ACTIVE` policy with a tighter scope (a new version, M-11 §12.11); *suspended* = `REVOKED` (revocation narrows, immediate); *invalid* = a `DRAFT`/`PROPOSED` that fails validation ⇒ never activated (stays `DRAFT` or is discarded). The canonical set is the frozen seven.

**1.** Policy. **2.** Policy. **3. Purpose.** A typed, versioned, scoped, deterministic posture evaluated at checkpoint step 6, returning a never-null gate decision. **4. Aggregate/txn.** Own aggregate; ### **a policy change is itself an M2 action class with `HUMAN_APPROVAL_REQUIRED` (no admin path).** **5. Owner.** the tenant's single named Policy Owner. **6. Initial.** `DRAFT`. **7. States.** the 7 (registry §4/M11). **8. Terminal.** `SUPERSEDED, REVOKED, EXPIRED`. **9. Non-terminal.** `DRAFT, PROPOSED, APPROVED, ACTIVE`. **10. Recoverable.** all non-terminal. **11. Failure.** validation failure ⇒ stays `DRAFT` (not activated). **12. Expiry.** `EXPIRED` — ### **only a NARROWING policy; its expiry BROADENS, so it requires a human at expiry.** **13. Unknown-outcome.** none.

## 15. Illegal transitions. ### **a model/automation activating or broadening policy → ILLEGAL (GR-7).** An `ACTIVE` policy with a null `gate_decision` → ILLEGAL. A tenant policy broadening the product ceiling → ILLEGAL. A predicate branching on `MODEL_INFERRED` → fails to compile (never reaches `ACTIVE`). Automatic broadening → ILLEGAL. **16. Precedence.** the precedence *of policies at evaluation* is Constraint > Permanent Truth > Brake > Product > Tenant(narrow-only) > Rules > Workflow-default (spec §20.7); ### **a policy never overrides a Permanent Truth or the Brake.** **17. Concurrency.** `UNIQUE(tenant,scope) WHERE state='ACTIVE'` + OCC. **18. Version-check.** `policy_version` monotonic; bound into every Witness/Grant. **19. Idempotency.** GR-4; re-activating an active version is a no-op. **20. Retry.** n/a. **21. Replay.** GR-11 — decisions are always explained under the version in force at their checkpoint.
## 22. Cancellation. `REVOKED` (PO-6). **23. Expiry.** PO-7 (narrowing only, human at expiry). **24. Reopening.** n/a. **25. Correction.** a wrong policy is superseded (retained). **26. Supersession.** PO-5. **27. Compensation.** n/a. **28. Human-approval.** PO-3/PO-4 (the change is itself gated). **29. Policy.** (self). **30. Brake.** ### **precedence: the brake denies regardless of what policy permits.**
## 31. Evidence/provenance. the predicate reads only deterministic inputs; ### **`confidence` is structurally not an input.** **32. Events.** PO-* per registry. **33. Consumes.** `HumanActivated, HumanRevoked, TimerFired`. **34. Writes.** policy version row + outbox; ### **`PolicyVersionChanged` voids in-flight approvals/witnesses/unclaimed grants** (their `policy_version` no longer current — enforced at PL-7v/PL-9v and the claim CAS). **35. Txn.** activation one commit. **36. Crash recovery.** the active version persists; ### **the policy engine unavailable at checkpoint ⇒ no decision ⇒ no witness ⇒ no effect (fail closed).** **37. Timeout.** PO-7. **38. Observability.** ### **every action class's gate decision visible on one screen (R17); override rate monitored.** **39. Audit.** every draft/activate/supersede/revoke/expire is an audit AND security event with actor + diff. **40. Security.** a model can never activate/broaden/reinterpret; inbound content can never author policy.
## 41. Acceptance. (a) an unregistered gate ⇒ system fails to start; (b) a predicate on `MODEL_INFERRED` fails to compile; (c) a tenant policy cannot broaden; (d) a policy change voids in-flight approvals; (e) evaluation is byte-identical reproducible.
