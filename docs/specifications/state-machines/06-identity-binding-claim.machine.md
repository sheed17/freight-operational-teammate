*Registry: `registry.md`. Entity: `entities/09-identity-binding-claim.md`. Lifecycle: Target Spec §12.6; ADR-007.*

**1.** Identity Binding Claim. **2.** Identity Binding Claim. **3. Purpose.** A correctable, native claim that an artifact belongs to an entity — identity as a first-class, evidenced, escalatable decision. **4. Aggregate/txn.** Own aggregate; a **correction** triggers event-driven propagation (not a mega-txn). **5. Owner.** system; a human on `AMBIGUOUS`/`CONFLICTING`. **6. Initial.** `PROPOSED`. **7. States.** the 7 (registry §4/M6). **8. Terminal.** `REJECTED, SUPERSEDED`. **9. Non-terminal human-owned.** `AMBIGUOUS, CONFLICTING`. **10. Recoverable.** `PROPOSED, CONFIRMED, CORRECTED`. **11. Failure.** `REJECTED`. **12. Expiry.** none. **13. Unknown-outcome.** none (identity uncertainty is `AMBIGUOUS`, not unknown).

## 15. Illegal transitions. ### **`OWNER_ASSERTED` + `RecomputedByInferrer` → ILLEGAL (IB-5x, the B3 regression).** `MODEL_INFERRED` → `CONFIRMED` → ILLEGAL (must be `AMBIGUOUS`). Two `CONFIRMED` for one subject → prevented by `UNIQUE(tenant,subject_ref) WHERE state='CONFIRMED'`. `MODEL_EXTRACTED` without an evidence span → ILLEGAL. Confirmation gated on a confidence score → ILLEGAL (confidence is not a guard input, GR-8). **16. Precedence.** a human assertion (IB-2h) and correction (IB-7) beat any inferrer recomputation; inferrer-vs-owner raises a Conflict rather than overwriting (IB-6). **17. Concurrency.** the `CONFIRMED`-uniqueness index + OCC. **18. Version-check.** GR-13 applies when a binding is a material fact of a consequential action (M2 step 4). **19. Idempotency.** GR-4; re-proposing an identical `LINKER_INFERRED` binding for a `CONFIRMED` subject is a no-op. **20. Retry.** the inferrer may re-run freely against `LINKER_INFERRED`; ### **never against `OWNER_ASSERTED`.** **21. Replay.** GR-11/12 — ### **every `OWNER_ASSERTED` binding survives replay byte-identical** (a rebuild rebuilds projections, not the owner's mind).
## 22. Cancellation. `REJECTED` (IB-8). **23. Expiry.** none. **24. Reopening.** n/a (a new claim is proposed). **25. Correction.** IB-7 propagates; ### **correction-of-correction supported, append-only, re-runs propagation from the newly-corrected value.** **26. Supersession.** IB-5 (linker) only. **27. Compensation.** IB-7 raises M10 for each completed effect that rested on a wrong binding (each individually gated; no bulk undo; aggregate exposure shown first). **28. Human-approval.** a `CONFIRMED` binding used by a money action is a material fact of that action's approval. **29. Policy.** ### **a policy/rule may never branch on a `MODEL_INFERRED` binding** (GR-8). **30. Brake.** binding continues under a brake (it is knowing, not acting); but a *consequential* action using the binding is admission-blocked.
## 31. Evidence/provenance. central; `provenance_class = f(match_method)` immutable (SD-6); `MODEL_EXTRACTED` requires an evidence span; no laundering (R-P2, GR). **32. Events.** IB-* per registry. **33. Crash recovery.** re-derive `LINKER_INFERRED` claims freely; `OWNER_ASSERTED` and human decisions are preserved from the event log. **37. Timeout.** none. **38. Observability.** `AMBIGUOUS` rate is the key onboarding metric (should fall); conflict count near zero; correction rate is the trust metric. **39. Audit.** every proposal/confirmation/correction/supersession/conflict with `provenance_class`, `match_method`, `decision_ref`. **40. Security.** `OWNER_ASSERTED` needs an authenticated human + immutable id; a model proposes, never confirms/resolves; counterparty claims are `MODEL_EXTRACTED` fraud signals.
## 41. Acceptance. (a) `MODEL_INFERRED` never confirms at confidence 1.0; (b) `OWNER_ASSERTED` survives a linker re-run (illegal on overwrite); (c) a single weak candidate is `AMBIGUOUS`; (d) correction propagates M10; (e) ordinal binding resolves to an immutable id or fails closed.
