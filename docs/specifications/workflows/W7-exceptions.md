*Registry defaults apply. ### **This is Neyma's core job — catch everything** (Operating Model L7).*

**2.** W7. **3.** Every exception, dispute, conflict, or recovery reaches an **accountable, decision-referenced resolution** — and never disappears because its originating loop moved on. **4.** ### **nothing falls through the cracks; the owner sees the assembled evidence and a proposed resolution.** **5.** own each exception until `RESOLVED{decision_ref}`. **6.** ### **NOT an error log — an Exception is unclosable without a decision (F-30); the originating loop advancing does NOT close it.** **10.** the exception's assigned human (from creation, I1). **13.** any entity; Exception, Conflict, Compensation, OS&D Case. **14.** `RESOLVE_EXCEPTION`.
