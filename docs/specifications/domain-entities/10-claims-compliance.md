# Domain Family 10 — Claims & Compliance *(Claim/OS&D Case · Compliance Record · Carrier Qualification Decision)*

*Registry applies. ### **A freight `Claim` (cargo loss/damage) is NOT an evidentiary `Claim` (Identity Binding) — always qualified; here `Claim` = OS&D Case.***

---
## E35 — Claim / OS&D Case · lifecycle **L-Claim**
**Def.** A cargo loss / damage / overage / shortage case (OS&D). **Not.** ### **Not an Identity Binding Claim** (the platform primitive); not an Exception (an OS&D Case *may raise* Exceptions). **Authority.** native. **Identifier.** `osd_case_id`. **Attrs.** `type ∈ {OVERAGE, SHORTAGE, DAMAGE, LOSS}`, `filed_by` (role), `amount_claimed` (money — direction depends on who claims), `evidence_refs[]` (POD notations, photos), `liable_party?`. **L-Claim states.** `OPENED → INVESTIGATING → RESOLVED` · `DENIED` · `ESCALATED`. **Key transitions:** `RESOLVED` requires a `decision_ref`; may drive a Compensation (a credit/deduction). ### **Hostile #23 — a claim opened AFTER the invoice was PAID ⇒ the invoice stays `PAID` (immutable history, GR-12); the claim resolution creates a NEW financial adjustment (credit/debit), never a rewrite of the paid invoice.** #17 (POD damaged, tracking normal) ⇒ opens an OS&D Case from the `ConflictRaised`. **Approvals.** a settlement/credit is money-affecting ⇒ `HUMAN_APPROVAL_REQUIRED`. **Acceptance.** `test_claim_after_paid_invoice_creates_adjustment_not_rewrite`; `test_osd_distinct_from_binding_claim`. **Invalid-impossible.** editing a paid invoice to reflect a claim. **Open.** claim workflow per customer/carrier contract — NEEDS VALIDATION.

## E36 — Compliance Record · lifecycle **L-Compliance**
**Def.** A time-bounded compliance fact about a Carrier — authority status, insurance/COI, safety signal. **Not.** ### **Not the Qualification Decision** (that is a native judgment *using* these records); not the Carrier. **Authority.** ### **FIELD-LEVEL:** `authority_status`/`safety_rating` — **projected from FMCSA** (freshness-checked); `insurance`/`coi` — projected from the insurer/COI (with `expires_at`); a `fraud_signal` — `MODEL_INFERRED` **surfaced, never decisive**. **Identifier.** `compliance_record_id`. **Time.** ### **every record carries `effective_at`/`expires_at`; freshness is decisive at qualification time.** **L-Compliance states.** `ACTIVE → EXPIRED` · `SUPERSEDED` · `FLAGGED`. **Key transitions:** `EXPIRED` (T, durable timer on `expires_at`) ⇒ ### **any dependent qualification must be re-evaluated (hostile #18: onboarded once, insurance expires before a later load — the OLD onboarding does NOT persist qualification, CD-2).** **CD-2 support.** **Acceptance.** `test_compliance_record_has_expiry`; `test_expired_insurance_forces_requalification`. **Invalid-impossible.** a compliance record without an `expires_at` gating a consequential tender. **Open.** FMCSA/insurer feed cadence — NEEDS VALIDATION.

---
## E37 — Carrier Qualification Decision · lifecycle **L-Qual**
**Def.** ### **A native, time-and-movement-specific JUDGMENT of whether a Carrier may be used** — evaluated at the relevant time, using Compliance Records. **Not.** ### **Not a Compliance Record** (raw facts); not permanent onboarding. **Authority.** ### **native — and the FINAL trust decision is HUMAN where the Operating Model reserves it (a model may SURFACE fraud/compliance indicators; it may NOT make the final trust decision).** **Identifier.** `qualification_decision_id`. **Attrs.** `carrier_id`, `evaluated_at`, `scope` (movement/lane/commodity), `inputs[]` (compliance record versions), `decision ∈ {QUALIFIED, NOT_QUALIFIED, CONDITIONAL}`, `decided_by` (human where reserved), `expires_at`. **L-Qual states.** `EVALUATING → QUALIFIED` · `NOT_QUALIFIED` · `CONDITIONAL` · `EXPIRED` · `SUSPENDED`. **Key transitions:** `QUALIFIED` (guard: authority active + insurance unexpired + no blocking safety/fraud signal, ### **at `evaluated_at`**); `SUSPENDED` (a new adverse signal) ⇒ ### **hostile #4: qualified at tender, suspended before pickup — a fresh evaluation at booking/pickup blocks (qualification is NOT sticky, CD-2).** **CD-2 (the invariant this entity exists for):** ### **a Movement cannot be tendered/booked to a carrier without a `QUALIFIED` (or human-approved) decision at that time.** **Security.** ### **a model surfaces indicators ⇒ `FraudSignalRaised`/narrow autonomy; the human makes the reserved trust call.** **Acceptance.** `test_qualification_is_time_and_movement_specific`; `test_model_surfaces_but_human_decides_trust`; `test_suspension_blocks_later_booking`. **Invalid-impossible.** a model making the final trust decision where reserved; treating onboarding as permanent qualification. **Open.** which trust decisions are human-reserved per tenant — NEEDS VALIDATION (fail-closed to human).
