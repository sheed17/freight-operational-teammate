# Domain Family 02 — Equipment & Facility

*Registry + conventions apply. Each entity complete/distinct.*

---
## E8 — Equipment
**Def.** A trailer/container/asset unit used on a movement (dry van, reefer, flatbed…). **Not.** The Carrier; not the Driver; not the Movement. **Authority.** field-level; `equipment_type`/`temp_requirements` — `OWNER_ASSERTED` (from the order) or `SYSTEM_IMPORTED` (TMS); `trailer_number` — `MODEL_EXTRACTED` from docs/tracking. **Identifier.** `equipment_id`. **External.** trailer number (untrusted, reused across carriers). **Collision.** ### **trailer numbers recycle — never a trusted identity.** **Enums.** `equipment_type ∈ {DRY_VAN, REEFER, FLATBED, STEP_DECK, POWER_ONLY, …}` (extensible via the action-class/registry, additive). **Time.** reefer `temp_requirements` carry no timezone; set-point is a value. **Relationships.** Carrier Movement 0:1 Equipment; Customer Order 0:1 `required_equipment`. **Fail-closed.** ### **a reefer load whose `temp_requirements` is `unknown` blocks autonomous dispatch** (a temp excursion is a claim). **Acceptance.** `test_trailer_number_is_not_trusted_identity`; `test_reefer_temp_required_before_dispatch`. **Open.** the equipment-type enum's customer-specific values — additive, NEEDS VALIDATION per customer.

## E9 — Facility
**Def.** A physical pickup/delivery location, **operated by a party that may not be the Customer** (a warehouse, a DC, a plant). **Not.** ### **Not a Customer Location by default** (a Consignee's DC is a Facility, not our Customer); not an Appointment. **Authority.** ### **FIELD-LEVEL:** `address` projected/`OWNER_ASSERTED`; ### **`timezone` — REQUIRED, from the address — the anchor for appointment-window evaluation (F-25)**; `hours_of_operation`/`appointment_required`/`lumper_required` — `OWNER_ASSERTED` or `MODEL_EXTRACTED` from prior loads; `dock_count` informational. **Identifier.** `facility_id`. **Natural.** geocoded address (medium collision — same building, many docks). **Collision.** ### **multi-tenant warehouses: one address, many businesses** — the facility is the *location*, the business is a *party role* at it. **Relationships.** Facility 1:N Stop; Facility 0:1 Customer Location. **Expectations.** ### **a facility's `appointment_required`/`lumper_required` drives Document Requirements and Appointment Expectations.** **Fail-closed.** ### **a Stop at a facility with an `unknown` timezone cannot have its appointment window evaluated ⇒ `INDETERMINATE`, never `OVERDUE` (CD-14, F-25).** **Acceptance.** `test_facility_timezone_required_for_window_eval`; `test_appointment_window_in_facility_local_time_across_dst`. **Invalid-impossible.** evaluating an appointment window in UTC or the tenant's timezone. **Open.** facility-attribute enrichment source — NEEDS VALIDATION.
