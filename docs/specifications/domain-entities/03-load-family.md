# Domain Family 03 — The Load Family *(Customer Order · Brokerage Load · Carrier Movement · Leg · Stop · Appointment)*

*Registry + conventions apply.*

> ### **THE LOAD-FAMILY RULE (preserved).** These are **DISTINCT concepts**. ### **There is NO generic `Load` entity.** Default cardinalities may be provisionally 1:1 where evidence is missing, but ### **identity is NOT collapsed.** The external TMS "load record" is represented **only** through **External Entity Mapping (#38)** — never as a domain entity.

## Load-family cardinality table
| Relationship | Confirmed | Provisional | Why it exists | Breaks if false | Migration if cardinality changes | NEEDS VALIDATION |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Customer Order → Brokerage Load | — | **1:1** | an order becomes a billable load | ### **hostile #1: one order → two loads (partial/split)** — a 1:1 model bills wrong | ### **the FK is on Load (`order_id`); a 1:N split adds Loads referencing one Order — no schema change, additive** | ✅ **YES** |
| Brokerage Load → Carrier Movement | — | **1:1** | a load is physically moved by a carrier | ### **hostile #2: re-power** (a load re-covered across two movements); **#3: multi-leg** | ### **the FK is on Movement (`load_id`); 1:N adds Movements referencing one Load** | ✅ **YES** |
| Carrier Movement → Leg | **1:N** | — | a movement has ≥1 physical segments | a 1:1 model can't express relay/team | Leg FK `movement_id` | confirmed |

| Stop → Appointment | **1:0..1** | — | a stop may need a scheduled window | — | Appointment FK `stop_id` | confirmed |

> ### **The 1:1 provisionals are recorded as PROVISIONAL with the migration path pre-specified — so a later 1:N discovery is additive, never a rewrite. Identity is never collapsed to make the 1:1 convenient.**

## E10 — Customer Order · lifecycle **L-Order**
**Def.** The **customer's request** to move freight — **before** it is a billable Brokerage Load. **Not.** ### **Not the Brokerage Load** (the order is intent; the load is our commercial unit); not a Quote. **Authority.** native (customer-sourced via a `CustomerOrder` observation or a human). **Identifier.** `order_id`. **External.** a customer PO number (untrusted — customer-scoped, collides across customers). **Attrs.** shipper/consignee/bill-to (party roles), commodity, weight, `required_equipment`, requested pickup/delivery windows (timezone-carrying). **Money.** a `quoted_uncommitted` sell target only. **L-Order states.** `RECEIVED → CONFIRMED → CONVERTED` · `CANCELLED`. **Key transitions:** `CONVERTED` (S) creates ≥1 Brokerage Load (guard: shipper+consignee+commodity `consistent`). **Reopen:** a cancelled order reopens as a **new** Order (GR-12). **Events:** `WorkItemCreated` (an order to cover), `ObservationBound`. **Fail-closed.** an `AMBIGUOUS` bill-to blocks conversion. **Acceptance.** `test_order_distinct_from_load`; `test_one_order_can_convert_to_two_loads` (#1). **Open.** Order→Load cardinality (NEEDS VALIDATION).

## E11 — Brokerage Load · lifecycle **L-Load**
**Def.** ### **The commercial unit we are paid for — one Customer obligation, one bill.** **Not.** ### **Not the Carrier Movement** (physical execution); not the TMS row (that is a mapping); not a generic `Load`. **Authority.** native. **Identifier.** `load_id` (uuid); ### **a human-facing `load_ref` (the number the owner says "bill load 4471") — customer/TMS-scoped.** **Collision.** ### **`load_ref` collides across tenants and across TMSs — trusted ONLY within `(tenant, TMS)` via External Entity Mapping.** **Money.** the **sell** side (money **IN**); margin = sell − Movement.buy. **L-Load states.** `TENDERED → COVERED → DISPATCHED → IN_TRANSIT → DELIVERED → DOCUMENTED → BILLED → PAID` · `CANCELLED` · `TONU`. ### **The loop closes at `PAID`, not `BILLED` (P24, CD-10).** **Key transitions:** `→DOCUMENTED` (guard: the Document Packet is `consistent` — **the POD gate, CD-3**); `→BILLED` (a consequential effect: `RAISE_INVOICE` via a Pipeline Instance — checkpoint + grant); `→PAID` (a verified Payment Application). **Reopen:** ### **a written-off/PAID load reopens as a new phase if a late POD/short-pay surfaces (V1, NEEDS VALIDATION); closure events immutable.** **Cancellation:** `CANCELLED` pre-dispatch; post-pickup ⇒ TONU/claim territory (#21). **Conflicts.** a Rate Con contradicting the committed sell ⇒ `ConflictRaised` (CD-16). **Approvals.** `RAISE_INVOICE` is gated; ### **`DELIVERED` does NOT imply POD (CD-8); `DOCUMENTED` does NOT imply billed (CD-9).** **Acceptance.** `test_load_distinct_from_movement_and_tms_row`; `test_load_closes_at_paid_not_billed`; `test_documented_requires_consistent_packet`. **Invalid-impossible.** billing without a `consistent` packet or an authorized exception (CD-3); a generic `Load` absorbing Order/Movement. **Open.** Load→Movement cardinality; reopen policy (V1).

## E12 — Carrier Movement · lifecycle **L-Move**
**Def.** ### **One carrier's physical execution of (part of) a load — what gets SETTLED with a carrier.** **Not.** The Brokerage Load (commercial); not a Leg. **Authority.** native. **Identifier.** `movement_id`. **Money.** the **buy** side (money **OUT**/`owed_to_carrier`). **L-Move states.** `OFFERED → BOOKED → CONFIRMED → PICKED_UP → DELIVERED → INVOICED → SETTLED` · `FELL_OFF` · `CANCELLED`. **Key transitions:** `BOOKED` (guard: ### **a `QUALIFIED` Carrier Qualification Decision at booking time OR human approval — CD-2**; `BOOK_CARRIER` is `HUMAN_APPROVAL_REQUIRED`); `SETTLED` (a verified Carrier Payable payment). **`FELL_OFF`:** ### **hostile #22 — a carrier falls off after appointment confirmation** ⇒ `FELL_OFF` ⇒ the Load re-covers via a **new** Movement (Load→Movement becomes 1:N). **Conflicts.** a late Rate Con contradicting a completed payable ⇒ `ConflictRaised` (#29). **CD-1:** a Carrier Assignment cannot be active without an active Movement. **Acceptance.** `test_move_distinct_from_load`; `test_book_requires_qualified_or_human`; `test_fell_off_recovers_via_new_movement`. **Open.** Load→Movement cardinality.

## E13 — Leg · lifecycle **L-Leg**
**Def.** A segment of a Movement between two Stops (relay/team/intermodal). **Not.** The Movement; not a Stop. **Authority.** native. **Identifier.** `leg_id`. **Cardinality.** Movement **1:N** Leg (confirmed). **L-Leg states.** `PLANNED → ACTIVE → COMPLETED` · `CANCELLED`. **Acceptance.** `test_movement_can_have_several_legs` (#3). **Open.** none.

## E14 — Stop · lifecycle **L-Stop**
**Def.** One scheduled appearance at one Facility — a pickup or delivery. **Not.** A Facility (the *location*); not an Appointment (the *time*). **Authority.** field-level; sequence/type native; arrival/departure `MODEL_EXTRACTED` from tracking (a **claim**). **Identifier.** `stop_id`. **Enums.** `stop_type ∈ {PICKUP, DELIVERY}`. **Time.** ### **all Stop windows evaluated in the Facility's local timezone (F-25).** **L-Stop states.** `SCHEDULED → ARRIVED → DEPARTED` · `MISSED` · `RESCHEDULED`. **Key transitions:** `ARRIVED`/`DEPARTED` from tracking are **claims** (`OBSERVATION` provenance), not proof (CD-15); a `detention_clock` starts on `ARRIVED` past the appointment window (facility-local). **Change-of-address:** ### **hostile #7 — a pickup address change after assignment** ⇒ Stop `RESCHEDULED` + a Conflict if it invalidates the buy rate (mileage change). **Acceptance.** `test_stop_window_facility_local_tz`; `test_arrival_is_a_claim_not_proof`. **Open.** none.

## E15 — Appointment · lifecycle **L-Appt**
**Def.** A **confirmed time window** at a Stop's Facility. **Not.** ### **Not the Stop** (a Stop may exist without an appointment); not a request. **Authority.** ### **FIELD-LEVEL:** the requested window — native; the **confirmed** window — **projected from the facility/portal** (an `ObservationBound`). **Identifier.** `appointment_id`. **Time.** ### **window in the FACILITY's local timezone + retained originating tz (F-25).** **L-Appt states.** `REQUESTED → CONFIRMED → KEPT` · `MISSED` · `RESCHEDULED` · `CANCELLED`. **CD-13:** ### **`REQUESTED` does NOT imply `CONFIRMED`** — an unconfirmed appointment cannot gate an autonomous dispatch. **Change-after-dispatch:** ### **hostile #15 — moved after dispatch** ⇒ `RESCHEDULED` + re-notify (a Work Item), + an Expectation re-versioned. **Expectations.** an Appointment creates a `check-call`/`arrival` Expectation (discharged by tracking; `INDETERMINATE` if tracking is blind — CD-14). **Acceptance.** `test_requested_appointment_does_not_confirm`; `test_appointment_move_after_dispatch_reschedules`. **Open.** portal appointment sources — NEEDS VALIDATION per facility.

## Family-wide
**Aggregate.** each entity its own aggregate; coordination event-driven. **Events.** lifecycle transitions map to platform events (a `→BILLED` is a `RAISE_INVOICE` Pipeline → `EffectVerified`+`ProjectionUpdated`). **Work Items.** an uncovered Load, an unconfirmed Appointment, a `FELL_OFF` Movement each originate a Work Item. **Brake.** `BOOK_CARRIER`/`RAISE_INVOICE` are admission-controlled. **Adversarial (family):** hostile #1, #2, #3, #7, #15, #21, #22, #25, #26 — traced in the review.
