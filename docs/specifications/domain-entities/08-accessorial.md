# Domain Family 08 — Accessorial *(Accessorial Charge · Accessorial Authorization)*

*Registry applies. ### **Charge and Authorization are DISTINCT and MUST NOT be collapsed.***

---
## E28 — Accessorial Charge · lifecycle **L-Access**
**Def.** A charge beyond the line-haul (detention, lumper, layover, TONU, reweigh, redelivery…) — ### **the highest-fraud-risk line item in freight.** **Not.** ### **Not the Authorization** (the charge is the *ask*; the authorization is the *right to bill/pay it*); not an Invoice/Payable line (it *feeds* them). **Authority.** native. **Identifier.** `accessorial_id`. **Attrs.** `charge_type`, `amount_minor`+`currency`+**`money_direction`** (a customer accessorial = IN; a carrier accessorial = OUT), `requesting_party` (role), `responsible_party` (role), `occurred_at` (UTC+tz), `evidence_refs[]` (scale ticket, lumper receipt, detention clock), `authorization_id?`. **Enums.** `charge_type ∈ {DETENTION, LUMPER, LAYOVER, TONU, REWEIGH, REDELIVERY, DETENTION_LOADING, …}` (additive). **L-Access states.** `CLAIMED → SUPPORTED → AUTHORIZED → BILLABLE/PAYABLE` · `DISPUTED` · `DENIED`. **Key transitions:** `SUPPORTED` (evidence present); `AUTHORIZED` (guard: ### **a valid Accessorial Authorization #29 exists**); `BILLABLE`/`PAYABLE` feeds an Invoice/Payable line. **CD-5:** ### **an accessorial supported ONLY by a counterparty's assertion of authorization is `unconfirmed` and BLOCKS the payable.** **Dispute.** `DISPUTED` ⇒ Conflict ⇒ human. **Acceptance.** `test_accessorial_distinct_from_authorization`; `test_detention_with_only_counterparty_assertion_blocks` (#12). **Invalid-impossible.** a `PAYABLE` detention with no Authorization. **Open.** charge-type/customer accessorial matrices — NEEDS VALIDATION.

## E29 — Accessorial Authorization · lifecycle **L-AccessAuth**
**Def.** ### **The RIGHT to bill/pay an accessorial — the record of WHO authorized it, HOW, and WITH WHAT SCOPE.** **Not.** ### **Not the Charge**; not a counterparty's claim that authorization exists. **Authority.** ### **native, HUMAN-ONLY for the undocumented case.** **Identifier.** `authorization_id`. **Attrs.** `authorization_source` (a contract term / a documented approval / **a human assertion**), `authorized_by` (actor), `scope` (charge_type + amount cap + load), `expires_at`, `evidence_ref?`. **Provenance/gate.** ### **`provenance_class`:** a **contractual/documented** authorization is `SYSTEM_IMPORTED`/`MODEL_EXTRACTED`-with-artifact; ### **an UNDOCUMENTED authorization is `OWNER_ASSERTED` and its action class is `PERMANENT_HUMAN_ASSERTION_REQUIRED` — a model can NEVER create it (CD-5, ADR-003, permanent, cannot graduate).** **L-AccessAuth states.** `ASSERTED → CONFIRMED` · `REJECTED` · `EXPIRED` · `REVOKED`. **Key transitions:** `CONFIRMED` — ### **for the undocumented case, ONLY via an authenticated human assertion (hostile #13: a human confirms the undocumented detention authorization ⇒ `OWNER_ASSERTED`, `decision_ref`); a counterparty "per our call you approved it" (hostile #12) is `MODEL_EXTRACTED` ⇒ `CounterpartySelfAuthorizationDetected` ⇒ blocks + fraud signal, NEVER `CONFIRMED`.** **CD-5 (the invariant this entity exists for).** **Acceptance.** `test_undocumented_authorization_is_human_only`; `test_counterparty_claim_never_confirms_authorization`; `test_human_can_confirm_undocumented_detention`. **Invalid-impossible.** ### **a model-created Authorization; promoting a counterparty claim to `CONFIRMED`.** **Open.** which accessorials are contractually pre-authorized per customer — NEEDS VALIDATION (fail-closed to human).
