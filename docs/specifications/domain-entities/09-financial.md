# Domain Family 09 — Financial *(Customer Invoice · Customer Invoice Line · Carrier Payable · Carrier Payable Line · Payment Application)*

*Registry + Money Model apply. ### **Money direction is EXPLICIT everywhere (CD-17). AR (IN) and AP (OUT) are distinct — never one "amount".***

---
## E30 — Customer Invoice · lifecycle **L-Invoice**
**Def.** ### **What WE bill the CUSTOMER — money IN (AR).** **Not.** ### **Not a Carrier's invoice to us** (that feeds a Payable); not a Payment. **Authority.** native. **Identifier.** `invoice_id`; **External.** a TMS invoice number — ### **the TMS may RENUMBER on edit ⇒ NEVER key idempotency on it** (idempotency is the commit_key, ADR-009). **Money.** total = Σ lines; **`money_direction=IN`/`money_kind=owed_by_customer`**. **L-Invoice states.** `ELIGIBLE → PREPARED → APPROVED → ISSUED → SENT → PAID` · `SHORT_PAID` · `DISPUTED` · `VOIDED` · `CREDITED`. ### **Closes at `PAID`, not `SENT` (CD-10, P24) — "invoice sent does not imply payment collected".** **Key transitions:** `ELIGIBLE` (guard: ### **POD-gated — the Document Packet is `COMPLETE` or an authorized exception, CD-3**); `ISSUED` is a consequential effect (`RAISE_INVOICE` Pipeline → checkpoint + grant + `EffectVerified`); `PAID` from a verified Payment Application. **Correction / credit-and-rebill.** ### **hostile #20 — a void/credit is a Compensation (a gated effect); a rebill is a NEW invoice (a distinct action class `REISSUE_INVOICE`, ADR-009 V8) — NOT the same effect repeated (avoids a credit-and-rebill double-bill).** **Short-pay.** ### **hostile #19 — `SHORT_PAID` ⇒ a Conflict/dispute + a residual AR; never silently closed.** **CD-9:** POD received does not imply invoice sent. **Duplicate prevention.** commit-once on the `RAISE_INVOICE` commit_key (the load, not the amount — ADR-009). **Acceptance.** `test_invoice_is_AR_IN`; `test_invoice_closes_at_paid`; `test_eligible_requires_complete_packet`; `test_credit_and_rebill_are_distinct_effects`; `test_short_pay_leaves_residual_ar`. **Invalid-impossible.** billing without a complete packet/exception; a rebill reusing the original commit_key. **Open.** V1 (late-POD re-bill), V8 (reissue action class), payment terms per customer — NEEDS VALIDATION.

## E31 — Customer Invoice Line
**Def.** One billed component (linehaul, fuel, an authorized accessorial). **Not.** The invoice; a raw accessorial (it references an `AUTHORIZED` one). **Authority.** native. **Identifier.** `invoice_line_id`. **Money.** IN; `rate_basis`, `qty`, `amount_minor`. **Guard.** ### **an accessorial line requires an `AUTHORIZED` Accessorial (via a valid Authorization) — CD-5.** **Acceptance.** `test_invoice_accessorial_line_requires_authorization`. **Open.** tax lines (if applicable) — NEEDS VALIDATION.

## E32 — Carrier Payable · lifecycle **L-Payable**
**Def.** ### **What WE owe a CARRIER — money OUT (AP). The highest-risk class.** **Not.** ### **Not the Rate Con** (evidence of terms); not the Customer Invoice (that is IN). **Authority.** native. **Identifier.** `payable_id`. **Money.** `money_direction=OUT`/`money_kind=owed_to_carrier`; ### **the remittance target may be a Factoring Company, not the Carrier (hostile #14) — `remittance_party` is explicit and verified.** **L-Payable states.** `INVOICE_RECEIVED → RECONCILED → APPROVED → RECORDED → PAID` · `DISPUTED` · `HELD` · `SHORT_PAID` · `DUPLICATE_SUPPRESSED`. ### **`RECORDED` does NOT imply `PAID` (CD-11).** **Key transitions:** `RECONCILED` (guard: ### **buy rate matches the Rate Con; accessorials `AUTHORIZED`; no `conflicting` field — CD-4**); `APPROVED` (### **money-out — `HUMAN_APPROVAL_REQUIRED`**); `RECORDED`/`PAID` are consequential effects (Pipeline). **CD-4:** ### **cannot become `APPROVED` with unresolved conflicting evidence (GR-10).** **Duplicate.** `DUPLICATE_SUPPRESSED` on a repeated carrier invoice number (occurrence_key). **Hostile #14 (paid to wrong factor):** ### **the `remittance_party` is a verified, `OWNER_ASSERTED`/documented binding — a factoring change is a consequential re-bind requiring evidence; paying the wrong factor is prevented by verifying `remittance_party` at the checkpoint.** **Acceptance.** `test_payable_is_AP_OUT`; `test_payable_recorded_not_paid`; `test_payable_blocked_on_conflicting_evidence`; `test_remittance_party_verified_before_pay`. **Invalid-impossible.** approving a payable with a `conflicting` material field; an undocumented accessorial on it (CD-5). **Open.** factoring-verification source — NEEDS VALIDATION.

## E33 — Carrier Payable Line
**Def.** One owed component (linehaul, fuel, authorized accessorial). **Not.** The payable. **Authority.** native. **Money.** OUT. **Guard.** an accessorial line requires an `AUTHORIZED` Accessorial (CD-5). **Acceptance.** `test_payable_accessorial_line_requires_authorization`. **Open.** none.

## E34 — Payment Application · lifecycle **L-PayApp**
**Def.** ### **The record of applying a received (AR) or sent (AP) payment to invoice(s)/payable(s) — cash application.** **Not.** The Invoice/Payable; not proof by itself of settlement direction without money_kind. **Authority.** ### **field-level:** the bank/remittance is `SYSTEM_IMPORTED` (projected); the application (which invoices) is native. **Identifier.** `payment_application_id`; **Natural.** the remittance reference (check no./ACH trace) — ### **the `occurrence_key` for payment idempotency (ADR-009); a partial payment is a distinct occurrence.** **Money.** direction explicit (a customer receipt = IN; a carrier disbursement = OUT). **L-PayApp states.** `RECEIVED → MATCHED → APPLIED` · `UNMATCHED` · `SHORT` · `OVER` · `REVERSED`. **Key transitions:** `APPLIED` closes an Invoice `→PAID` / a Payable `→PAID`; `SHORT`/`OVER` ⇒ residual + Conflict/dispute; `REVERSED` (a bounced payment) ⇒ Compensation-adjacent (reopen the AR/AP). **CD-10/CD-11:** ### **this is the ONLY entity whose verified `APPLIED` closes the AR/AP loop — an invoice sent / a payable recorded do NOT.** **Acceptance.** `test_only_verified_payment_application_closes_the_loop`; `test_partial_payment_is_distinct_occurrence`; `test_reversal_reopens_ar`. **Invalid-impossible.** closing an invoice to `PAID` without a verified Payment Application. **Open.** bank-feed source + matching rules — NEEDS VALIDATION.
