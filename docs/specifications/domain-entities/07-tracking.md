## E27 — Tracking Event · lifecycle **L-Track**
**Def.** An observed movement signal — a position, a status assertion, a check-call, a delay — from a source. **Not.** ### **Not proof of real-world completion** (CD-15); not the Stop; not the Appointment. **Authority.** ### **FIELD-LEVEL and SOURCE-SPECIFIC — the whole point:** distinguish `observed_status` · `driver_assertion` · `carrier_assertion` · `tms_status` · `tracking_provider_position` · `derived_eta`. Each carries its own `provenance_class`: a **position** from an ELD provider is `SYSTEM_IMPORTED`; a **driver "delivered"** is `MODEL_EXTRACTED` (a claim); a **derived ETA** is ### **`MODEL_INFERRED` — a Claim, NEVER projected fact and NEVER a gate (GR-8).** **Identifier.** `tracking_event_id`; **Natural.** `(tenant, source, external_id, content_digest)` (source-natural, idempotent). **Time.** UTC + tz; ### **positions carry the observation instant, not "now".** **Enums.** `status ∈ {AT_PICKUP, LOADED, IN_TRANSIT, AT_DELIVERY, DELIVERED, DELAYED, …}` (additive). **L-Track states.** `RECEIVED → BOUND` · `UNBOUND` · `SUPERSEDED` *(same shape as Observation — it IS an Observation specialization; not a new machine).* **CD-14:** ### **tracking UNAVAILABLE does NOT imply late or on-time** — a blind window ⇒ the Stop/Appointment Expectation goes `INDETERMINATE`, never `OVERDUE` (I8). **CD-8:** ### **`DELIVERED` status does NOT imply POD received** (distinct from L-Doc). **Detention clock.** starts on an `AT_DELIVERY`/`ARRIVED` past the appointment window (facility-local) — a **claim** basis for a detention Accessorial, requiring authorization to bill. **Hostiles:** #16 (tracking says delivered but POD missing) ⇒ `DELIVERED` claim + a POD Expectation still open ⇒ cannot bill (CD-3, CD-8); #17 (POD says damaged but tracking says normal) ⇒ `ConflictRaised` (readback-vs-claim) ⇒ human + possible Claim/OS&D. **Acceptance.** `test_derived_eta_is_model_inferred_claim`; `test_tracking_unavailable_is_indeterminate`; `test_delivered_status_does_not_imply_pod`; `test_delivered_vs_missing_pod_blocks_billing`. **Invalid-impossible.** a `MODEL_INFERRED` ETA gating an autonomous action; a status field proving completion for a consequential effect. **Open.** tracking-provider trust tiers — NEEDS VALIDATION.
